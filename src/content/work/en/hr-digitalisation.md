As an IT Consultant at **Onespire**, I served **Wizz Air** as primary client, leading the digitalisation of the recruitment side of their HR processes.

> Part of Wizz Air's multi-million-USD **2030 strategic programme**, spanning recruitment processes for roughly 1,000 employees.

**What I delivered:**

- Full **requirement gathering, stakeholder interviews and GAP analysis** for the recruitment workstream.
- Optimised onboarding workflows and identified concrete digitalisation potential across the process.
- Functional specifications authored hand-in-hand with development teams to ensure delivery met business intent.
- Validation and refresh of back-office HR processes for the **Wizz Air Malta** entity launch — a high-stakes new subsidiary requiring rapid process readiness.

The work translated complex, high-volume HR operations into clear, deployable requirements that fed directly into a strategic enterprise programme.
