Within my Production & HSE portfolio at **MOL Group**, I delivered the **HSE digitalisation roadmap** — a ~$500K programme that brought health, safety and environment processes into the digital age.

> The first standardised digital HSE workflow across the Group's Hungarian, Slovakian and Croatian sites — for both blue- and white-collar staff.

**Flagship deliveries:**

- A **waste-management** solution and **HSE mobile applications** — an in-house HSE Info App alongside the Enablon SE / Wolters Kluwer EHS platform mobile app.
- Group-level harmonisation of HSE processes, replacing inconsistent, site-by-site practices with one standardised digital workflow.
- Requirements captured directly from the plant floor — explicitly including blue-collar use cases after smartphones were rolled out to all employees.

This work made safety reporting and compliance faster, more consistent and accessible to every employee, regardless of role or location.
