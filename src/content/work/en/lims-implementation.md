As Downstream IT Senior Domain Expert at **MOL Group**, I drove a Group-wide **Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS)** implementation — one of the flagship deliveries within my Production, HSE & Laboratory portfolio.

> A multi-year programme worth ~$3M, covering roughly 500 laboratory staff across Hungary, Slovakia and Croatia.

The goal was to replace fragmented, site-specific lab practices with a single harmonised system and consistent data flows across the Group's downstream footprint.

**What I owned:**

- Requirement gathering and process harmonisation across multiple refinery and petrochemical laboratories in three countries.
- Stakeholder alignment from laboratory specialists on the plant floor up to Group leadership.
- Vendor management, scoping and multi-year roadmap planning, including CAPEX/OPEX governance and budget defence.
- Coordination of the delivery from early ideation through requirements and rollout hand-over.

The result: standardised laboratory processes and trustworthy, comparable data across the Group — a foundation for higher-quality decisions in daily operations.
