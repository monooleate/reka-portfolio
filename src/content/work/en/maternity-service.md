As Product Owner at **Medicover**, I owned the product backlog and business requirements for an outpatient and inpatient **process-optimisation platform**, and led the IT-side launch of a brand-new private maternity service.

> A ~$200K platform serving roughly 200 physicians and several thousand patients.

**What I owned:**

- The full product backlog and business requirements for the clinical platform.
- The IT-side introduction of a **new private maternity service line** — coordinating system readiness, process design and release across clinical and operational stakeholders.
- The product roadmap, agile delivery cadence and release coordination.
- Direct collaboration with **physicians as domain experts** to gather, refine and validate requirements that reflect real clinical workflows.

The result was a smooth launch of a new service line, built on a platform that genuinely fit how clinicians work.
