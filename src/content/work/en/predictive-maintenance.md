As a Process Information Engineer at **MOL's Danube Refinery**, I designed and deployed predictive-maintenance analytics on the **OSIsoft PI System**, bringing reliability and asset-integrity thinking into daily refinery operations.

> A business case that identified **$500–800K** of annual savings potential through fewer unplanned events and stronger operational excellence.

**What I built:**

- **Integrity Operating Window (IOW)** monitoring, corrosion and thickness trending, and automated early-warning logic to prevent integrity-related failures.
- Operational dashboards and business logic for daily throughput and yield tracking, off-spec prevention and process-stability monitoring.
- Mass and energy balance reconciliation support using Pimsoft Sigmafine.
- The introduction of **machine-learning–driven decision support** into operational workflows.

These tools became part of the daily routine for operators, technologists and integrity engineers on the plant floor.
